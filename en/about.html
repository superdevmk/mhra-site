Mission

MHRA’s mission is to support the development of modern HR practices, enable exchange of knowledge, experience and tools, and contribute to a work environment where people are in focus.

Vision

The association’s vision is to become a reference point for HR professionals and organizations, recognized for its impact on the quality of human resources management.
